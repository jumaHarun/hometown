Top 4 Activities to do in Lamu Town

Tusitiri Dhow

An uncommonly luxurious and genuine way to experience the Lamu archipelago. Spend nights under the stars while enjoying delectable regional cuisine.

Amu Ranch

A breathtaking wilderness of coastal forest and Savannah woodland that serves as an important migratory corridor and wildlife habitat for a variety of endemic animal species, including the Somali Lion and Aders Duiker as well as uncommon marine life and a wide diversity of birds.

The Fort of Shela

A stunning and unique haven that provides seclusion and individualized hospitality in a tranquil and serene setting. Nestled among the sand dunes at Shela Beach, this building serves as a tribute to Omani architectural genius.

Lamu Museum

This double-story medieval mansion, completed in 1892 and located on a waterfront, was originally home of Jack Haggard, Queen Victoria's consul. A recreated Swahili home, artifacts from Takwa, and various siwa, or dhow ceremonial horns, are among the Swahili culture exhibits on display.
